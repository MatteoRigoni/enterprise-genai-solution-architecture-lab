1) What is AcmeCloud?

AcmeCloud is a cloud storage service that lets users upload, organize, and share files securely across devices.

2) How do I reset my password?

Go to Settings → Security → Reset Password.
You will receive an email with a reset link valid for 15 minutes.

3) What file types are supported?

AcmeCloud supports common formats such as PDF, DOCX, JPG, PNG, MP4, and ZIP.
Executable files (like .exe) are blocked.

4) What is the storage limit on the free plan?

The free plan includes 5 GB of total storage.

6) How long are deleted files kept?

Deleted files remain in the Trash for 30 days, then are permanently removed.

7) Does AcmeCloud offer refunds?

Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable.
Yearly subscriptions can be refunded within 14 days of purchase if usage is under 1 GB.

8) How do I contact support?

Support is available Monday–Friday, 9:00–18:00 UTC via in-app chat and email.
